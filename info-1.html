Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the K801 Handheld Fan.

How long does the battery last on a full charge?

The battery life depends on the speed setting you use. On the lowest setting (level 1), the fan can run for up to 12 hours. On medium settings (around level 50), expect about 6-8 hours. At maximum speed (level 100), the fan will run for approximately 4 hours.

How do I clean the fan?

First, make sure the fan is turned off and unplugged. Use a soft, dry cloth to wipe the exterior. For the blades, you can use a small brush or compressed air to remove dust. Avoid using water or cleaning solutions as they may damage the electrical components.

Can I use the fan while it's charging?

Yes, the K801 supports pass-through charging, meaning you can use it while it's plugged in. However, for optimal battery health, we recommend occasional full discharge/charge cycles.

What's included in the box?

Each K801 Handheld Fan comes with: 1 x Handheld Fan, 1 x USB-C charging cable, 1 x User manual, and 1 x Wrist strap. The package does not include a power adapter.
